Why local

Your voice stays yours

Cloud dictation uploads your microphone to someone else's servers and bills you monthly. WISPERMO transcribes on your own CPU, offline, for free. No subscription, no account, no data leaving the machine.

FAQ
Does it work offline?

Yes. The speech model downloads once, then everything runs on your machine with no network access.

What are the hardware requirements?

Any modern x86-64 CPU. The default base model needs ~1 GB RAM and transcribes a few seconds of speech in under a second. A GPU is optional.

Wayland or X11?

Both. Typing into other apps uses ydotool; on GNOME/Wayland a one-time permission step is handled by the setup wizard.

Which languages?

90+, via Whisper. Auto-detected, or pin one in settings for speed and accuracy.

What data do you collect?

None. There is no telemetry, no analytics, and no network calls beyond the one-time model download.
